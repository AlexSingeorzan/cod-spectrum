# Legal and platform terms

`cod-spectrum` defaults to recording a public VOD URL and its metadata. It processes a local file supplied by the operator. Downloading is disabled for configured sources unless that source explicitly sets `download: true`, and direct URL processing is an explicit download action.

Platform terms of service, broadcast rights, copyright rules, and permitted forms of analysis vary by platform, territory, account, and use case. The operator is responsible for confirming that they may download, retain, transform, and redistribute a VOD or generated clip. This project does not bypass access controls and does not grant rights to third-party content.

Keep downloads rate-limited, retain only the data needed for the permitted purpose, respect takedown requests, and do not publish generated clips without the required rights.
